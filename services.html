Quantum-Enhanced Machine Learning (QML) Solutions

Quantum-enhanced machine learning (QML) is a cutting-edge approach that uses quantum computing principles to accelerate machine learning tasks, such as classification, pattern recognition, and optimization. By harnessing quantum algorithms like the Quantum Support Vector Machine (QSVM) or Quantum Neural Networks (QNN), QML can significantly improve processing power and efficiency for complex datasets.

Service: Qiskit Company: IBM

Quantum Cryptography Solutions

Quantum cryptography leverages the principles of quantum mechanics to develop secure communication systems that are fundamentally immune to eavesdropping. The most well-known application is Quantum Key Distribution (QKD), which allows two parties to exchange encryption keys securely, even if an adversary is present.

Service: Azure Quantum Company: Microsoft
